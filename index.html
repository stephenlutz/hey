About Me

Stephen Lutz is a Manhattan-based DJ and graphic designer known for bold visuals and curated sound. Working with artists such as Michaela Jaé and Kyle Gordon, he creates everything from album artwork to live DJ sets. Whether through design or music, his work is thoughtful, polished, and made to stand out on the screen as well as the dancefloor.
