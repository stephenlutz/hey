About Me

Hi, I’m Stephen—a DJ and graphic designer based in Manhattan, blending visuals and sound to create unforgettable experiences. Whether I’m spinning tracks that get the room moving or designing bold, futuristic visuals, my work is all about capturing energy and turning it into something you can feel. As a designer, I specialize in album artwork, event flyers, and brand visuals that stand out—whether it's a minimal aesthetic or a hyper-digital vibe. I’ve had the chance to work on projects like the album cover for Michaela Jaé, where I designed both the typography and back cover layout for vinyl. My process is intuitive and collaborative, drawing from what makes each artist or brand unique. As a DJ, I’m all about building a vibe. From underground techno to experimental pop to throwback anthems, I love curating sounds that transform a space. I’ve played sets across NYC, and I’m always looking for new ways to mix genres and surprise a crowd. Looking for an album cover, a visual identity, or a DJ for your next event? Let’s make it happen.
